This is a project for my final-year dissertation. It involves simulating a hospital organisation that needs to manage services such as booking appointments and viewing future appointments, with the option to cancel them. The internal hospital processes – those that do not take place within the web application – are simulated using classes, such as the “Doctor” class, which is responsible for conducting consultations. To see how the site works, simply scroll through the options on the left. Thank you!
